What is Lunar Light Point?

  • LLP is a Record Label that seeks underground artists from all around the music spectrum.
  • We (well, I) don't take any monetary cuts from artists unless they make a lot of money.
  • All rights for music signed with LLP are of the artists.
  • But that's standard practices. What separates LLP from other labels?
  • LLP has a (currently very limited) community, made up of "Divisions".
  • These Divisions are based off genre, and will help artists find other people to work with.
  • This project aims to connect artists directly, through Discord, and see music thrive.
  • If you're interested in joining, more updates will be announced in the upcoming Discord server. You caught this project quite early.
